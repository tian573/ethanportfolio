About Me

Ethan Stever, born in Seattle, Washington on February 20, 1990, is a distinguished artist with a robust educational background. He completed his Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) in Painting at the University of Washington and later earned a Master of Fine Arts (MFA) in Visual Arts from the California Institute of the Arts.

Currently, Ethan works as a Professional Artist at Studio Azure in Los Angeles, California, specializing in contemporary painting with a focus on large-scale landscapes and abstract art. If you are seeking a talented and visionary artist to bring creativity and originality to your project, look no further than Ethan Stever. His diverse portfolio and expertise make him an ideal candidate to transform your artistic visions into reality.

Landscape Art

Landscape art is a genre of art that captures natural scenery such as mountains, valleys, trees, rivers, and forests. The main subject of landscape art is the depiction of the great outdoors, but it can also include elements such as buildings, people, and animals. This art form often aims to evoke the beauty, mood, and essence of a particular setting. Landscape art can be created using various mediums like painting, drawing, photography, and digital art.

Abstract Art

Abstract art is a style of art that doesn't attempt to represent reality or concrete objects directly. Instead, it uses shapes, colors, forms, and gestural marks to achieve its effect. This type of art emphasizes the artist's expression and interpretation, often leaving the viewer to derive their own meaning from the work.

Silhouette Art

Silhouette art is a form of artwork that involves creating an image, often of a person's profile or an object, in solid black against a lighter background. The focus is on the outline of the subject, capturing its shape and contours without any internal details. This style creates a striking contrast and emphasizes the form of the subject in a bold and dramatic way.
